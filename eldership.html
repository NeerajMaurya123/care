Eldership - A Senior’s Health Integrated Program
Ageing is inevitable. Everyone in this world ages differently, according to their physical structure and climatic surroundings.

Ageing is inevitable. Everyone in this world ages differently, according to their physical structure and climatic surroundings. Population over the age of 50 years has tripled in the last 40 years in India and will relentlessly increase in the future. Hence, it is imperative to treat ageing as normal and adapt a healthy lifestyle.

The growing nuclear family culture augments the need for a well-designed, comprehensive elder care program that includes preventive, curative and rehabilitative aspects of health. A care program that reduces dependency and helps manage physical, emotional, and social well-being of our elders.

Presenting eldership: An integrated health care service for elders at home.

Eldership - by Bonanza Care Home Care is a Senior’s Health Integrated Program that addresses, manages & prevents illness, while ageing at various stages of life. Our aim is to keep our elders healthy at home by regularly monitoring their health through medical, social and emotional well-being and help them age with grace and dignity.

We, at Bonanza Care Homecare are committed to helping elders stay in good health and maintain their independence in the comfort of their home.

Our well trained medics, customized care plans and state-of-the art technology ensures superior health care for elders. We are also of the ideology that no person should visit a hospital unless deemed necessary.

Our preventive health care measures influenced with futuristic technology makes sure we bring world class expertise to your home and offer you the most comprehensive health care services through the Eldership program.

  • Health Manager

    A dedicated Health Manager is assigned to manage the Eldership care plan and also facilitate any other care services required like -specialist consultation, nursing care, diagnostics, physiotherapy, etc.

  • Physician Consultation

    A personal Physician who will give consultation through a combination of home visits and virtual consultations covering medical and care needs.

  • Dietician Consultation

    The Dietician would provide a customized diet plan for Nutrition benefits and will also monitoring to establish healthy eating habits.

  • Health Coach

    A well-ness expert who will use various, participating engagement techniques and education sessions aimed at motivation and well-ness towards psychological well-being.

  • Geri Coach

    A Geri Coach will do a weekly monitoring call and share weekly / monthly health status reports. He will share an incident report with the Health Manager in case of any abnormal values so that the same can be addressed.

Your Well Being At Home, Is Our Aim At Workplace

Our team has been built to ensure that our patients receive the best possible care and timely follow-ups for their health evaluation.
