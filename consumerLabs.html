How it Works?

  • Search for required Lab tests/Healthcheckup and select Diagnostic centre of your choice. Add prescription, patient details and address to complete the booking.

  • Visit the lab centre as per your booking. For home sample collection, certified professional will collect the samples at your home.

  • We will email you the reports. You can also access your reports within your account on the Bonanza Care App.

You must follow the mentioned practices before a Health Checkup:
  • Unless otherwise specified, you must fast overnight for 12 hours before the appointment. Only water can be consumed.
  • Avoid smoking or consuming alcohol for a minimum of 24 hours before the Health Checkup.
  • Women should not undergo Health Checkups at the time of menstruation.
  • Pregnant women should not undertake X-Ray.
  • You may carry urine & stool samples in a sterilized container obtainable from the nearest pharmacy. (if applicable)
