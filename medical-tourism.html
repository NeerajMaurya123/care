Medical Tourism

Medical tourism refers to diverse practice of travelling across the globe to seek healthcare services. These services may vary from selective procedures to complex surgeries. In the past few decades, India has emerged as a hub of medical tourism. The following factors have contributed for the same.

  • Indian doctors and surgeons are world renowned and have been trained at the top medical institute in the US, Europe, or the developed nations.
  • Most medical professionals are professionally eloquent.
  • Indian hospitals included top-of-the-line medical and diagnostic equipment from global international conglomerates.
  • India offers luxury medical amenities at affordable prices.


Dedicated executive

A dedicated executive would be assigned once we get the confirmation of the patient and the attender travel and will be responsible in organizing the requirements at the hospital and outside the hospital for other Medical Assistance in India. The executive of Bonanza Healthcare will take the responsibility of booking the appointments at the hospital, interacting with the doctors, assisting to book the hotel/service apartment/guest house, organizing the local transport, etc and will take up complete responsibility in ensuring the satisfaction of the patient and the attender during the stay. The executive will also take responsibility to regularly update on the treatment taking place at the hospital, back home to their other relatives & family members.

Transportation

We will provide the assistance in organizing for a vehicle for all your local transfers within the city to make it easy for you to travel and also identify places without difficulty. Based on the proximity of the location we will also suggest you other modes of transport which can be convenient and safe.

Telecommunication aids

We will assist you in getting a contact number with the local service provider so you can keep in touch with your family & friends back home. We can also assist you in getting an internet dongle if required so you can keep connected to the internet and your emails as well.

Medical VISA to India- Complete Information

Bonanza Medical Tourism in India is all about the progress these days. The industry is growing at a rate that was unimaginable for years, some share of credit goes to the government of India for introducing a medical visa for foreign nationals. A visa on arrival system is introduced for a few selected countries which allow the patients to stay for 60 days and get world-class medical treatments in India.

Airport pickup

On receiving the patient’s travel itinerary our executive will organize for a transport and receive the patient/family at the airport and will support you in getting you to the desired place, could be either the hospital or the hotel/service apartment. Our executives will ensure that you have settled in comfortably and will also provide the assistance for other needs that you request for.

Accommodation

We have a tie up with most of the hotels, service apartments and guest houses nearby the hospitals so the patient’s family can have a comfortable stay outside the hospital during the treatment of the patient. Our executives will book the rooms as per your budget after a detailed discussion and will also be able to provide you with options which you can choose from as per your convenience.

Post-treatment care

Bonanza Healthcare executives will keep in touch with the patient and family members after returning back to your country. We will also facilitate to keep a regular communication between you and the doctors so there is a regular touch for your post treatment.

PRIVATE NURSING CARE

After the patient gets discharged from the hospital we can provide nursing care assistance to take care of the patient. The nursing care person will be identified as per the requirement and will provide complete personal and medical care to the patient for complete recovery before travelling back to your country.
