About Bonaza Care

The best investment you can make is in your health, because BonanzaCare is committed to making you as healthy as possible.

Bonanza Medical Tourism has agreements with many hospitals and clinics, as well as pathological laboratories and medical investigation centres that can provide specialized care for patients. The company also has its own network of service providers, such as travel agencies and other businesses that can help make medical tourism easier and more convenient.

Bonanza Medical Tourism is a company that helps people travel to medical facilities domestic and overseas. Bonanza Group was founded in 1994, and has since grown to be one of the largest financial services and broking companies in India. In addition to helping people with their health, Bonanza Medical Tourism (sister concern of Bonanza)also helps people live their lives on their own terms.

We want to work together to help develop the medical tourism industry so that people in need can get care from doctors in other countries. We think this will benefit all of us, as well as the patients.

Our Mission
We try to make the health system work better so that everyone can get the care they need at a good price. We work with health care professionals and other partners to make it easier for people to get the care they need. We support the doctor-patient relationship and want to help people make informed decisions about their own health.
